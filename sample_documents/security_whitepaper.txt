SECURITY WHITEPAPER

CERTIFICATIONS
The platform holds SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications, both
renewed annually through independent third-party audits. Audit reports
are available to Enterprise customers under NDA upon request.

ENCRYPTION
Customer data at rest is encrypted using AES-256. Data in transit between
clients and the platform, and between internal services, is encrypted
using TLS 1.3. Encryption keys are managed through a dedicated key
management service with automatic rotation every 90 days.

DATA RETENTION AND DELETION
Deleted documents are retained in a recovery bin for 14 days before
permanent deletion, allowing customers to recover accidentally deleted
files. After the 14-day window, data is permanently and irrecoverably
purged from primary and backup storage within an additional 30 days.

BACKUP AND DISASTER RECOVERY
The platform performs automated daily backups retained for 30 days and
maintains a multi-region failover setup with a recovery point objective
(RPO) of under 1 hour and a recovery time objective (RTO) of under 4
hours in the event of a regional outage.

COMPLIANCE FOR REGULATED INDUSTRIES
The platform supports HIPAA-compliant configurations for customers
handling healthcare-related data, available exclusively on Enterprise
plans with a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA). GDPR data
processing addenda are available for all customers operating in the
European Union.

ACCESS CONTROL
Enterprise plans support single sign-on (SSO) via SAML 2.0 and OIDC, as
well as role-based access control (RBAC) with custom permission sets.
All administrative actions are logged in an immutable audit trail
retained for 7 years.
