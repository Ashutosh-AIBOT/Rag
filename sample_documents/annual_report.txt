ANNUAL REPORT 2025

FINANCIAL HIGHLIGHTS
Fiscal year 2025 revenue was $158 million with a net profit margin of 14%,
driven primarily by growth in the Enterprise segment. Total revenue in Q3
2025 alone was $42.7 million, up 18% year over year. Operating expenses
grew more slowly than revenue, improving overall operating leverage.

SEGMENT PERFORMANCE
The Enterprise customer segment grew 34% year-over-year in 2025, becoming
the largest contributor to total revenue at 52% of the total. The
mid-market segment grew 11%, while the small-business segment remained
roughly flat, growing only 2% due to increased churn.

RISK FACTORS
Key risk factors disclosed for the upcoming fiscal year include foreign
currency exposure from international operations, dependency on a
relatively small number of large enterprise customers who together
represent a meaningful share of revenue, and increasing competition in
the mid-market segment from newer, lower-priced entrants.

OUTLOOK
Management expects continued double-digit revenue growth in fiscal year
2026, with investment concentrated in expanding the Enterprise sales team
and further developing the advanced analytics product line. The company
does not expect material margin expansion in the near term due to planned
infrastructure investments.

CAPITAL ALLOCATION
The board approved a $50 million share repurchase program during 2025.
No dividends were declared. Cash and cash equivalents at year end totaled
$210 million, providing ample runway for continued investment.
