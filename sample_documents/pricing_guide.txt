PRICING GUIDE

OVERVIEW
This document describes the subscription plans, pricing, billing policies,
and refund process for the platform.

STARTER PLAN
The Starter plan is designed for individuals and small teams getting started.
It costs $9 per month and includes 10GB of storage, email support with a
48-hour response time, and access to core features. The Starter plan allows
60 requests per minute against the API.

PROFESSIONAL PLAN
The Professional plan costs $49 per month and includes 100GB storage,
priority support with a 4-hour response time, and access to advanced
analytics dashboards. The Professional plan allows 300 requests per minute
against the API.

ENTERPRISE PLAN
The Enterprise plan is billed at $299 per month and includes unlimited
storage, single sign-on (SSO) integration, a dedicated account manager, and
24/7 phone support. Enterprise customers can negotiate custom API rate
limits per their contract. Enterprise storage tier costs $0.023 per gigabyte
per month for usage beyond the included allocation.

ANNUAL BILLING DISCOUNT
Customers who choose annual billing instead of monthly billing receive a
20% discount on their total subscription cost. Annual plans are billed
once per year and are non-refundable except in the case of service
unavailability exceeding the SLA.

REFUND POLICY
Refunds can be requested within 30 days of purchase by contacting support
with the invoice number. Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business
days back to the original payment method. Refunds are not available for
add-on usage charges already consumed during the billing period.

ADD-ONS
Additional storage beyond plan limits is billed at $0.03 per GB for
Starter and Professional plans. Additional seats can be purchased at $15
per seat per month on the Professional plan.
